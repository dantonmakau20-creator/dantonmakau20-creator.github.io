About

Educator. Innovator.
Change-Maker.

Danton Makau is a dynamic Educational Technology Professional based in Kenya, holding a Bachelor of Education (Arts) with Information Technology from Maseno University. He stands at the intersection of pedagogy and innovation — equipping young people with the tools, skills, and mindset needed to thrive in a technology-driven world.

With over three years of hands-on experience, Danton has facilitated robotics programs, coding bootcamps, STEM outreach activities, and AI-assisted learning workshops across more than 20 schools in Kenya. His work reaches urban centers and underserved communities alike, ensuring that technology education is inclusive, accessible, and inspiring.

As a trained English and Literature educator, Danton uniquely blends the art of communication with the science of technology — helping learners not only build robots and write code, but also think critically, articulate ideas, and lead with confidence. He is a regular presence at national hackathons, innovation showcases, and youth empowerment forums as both a facilitator and a mentor.

Danton's mission is simple but profound: to transform African classrooms into laboratories of innovation, where every student becomes a creator, a problem-solver, and a future-ready citizen.
