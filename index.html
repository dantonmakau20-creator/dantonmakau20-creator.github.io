Chapter 01 · The Educator

Technology is most powerful when it creates confidence.

Danton Makau is an Educational Technology Professional based in Kenya, holding a Bachelor of Education (Arts) with Information Technology from Maseno University. He works at the intersection of pedagogy, innovation, communication, and youth development.

His work spans robotics training, coding bootcamps, STEM outreach, hackathon mentorship, AI-assisted lesson planning, English and Literature instruction, and public speaking.

Danton’s mission is to transform African classrooms into laboratories of innovation where every learner becomes a creator, problem-solver, and future-ready citizen.
