About

Educator. Innovator.
Change-Maker.

Danton Makau is a dynamic Educational Technology Professional based in Kenya, holding a Bachelor of Education (Arts) with Information Technology from Maseno University. He stands at the intersection of pedagogy and innovation.

With hands-on experience, Danton has facilitated robotics programs, coding bootcamps, STEM outreach activities, and AI-assisted learning workshops across Kenyan schools and youth programs.

As a trained English and Literature educator, Danton blends communication with technology — helping learners build robots, write code, think critically, articulate ideas, and lead with confidence.

Danton's mission is to transform African classrooms into laboratories of innovation where every learner becomes a creator, problem-solver, and future-ready citizen.
